# DeenUp — Product Context for Blog CTAs

## About DeenUp

**DeenUp** is the first AI rooted in Quranic values — an Islamic companion app designed to help Muslims strengthen their faith through technology that respects and honors Islamic scholarship.

**Mission**: AI is shaping the future, but whose values will shape AI? Nearly every AI today is built by tech companies with little regard for the ummah. At DeenUp, we're building an alternative — AI that starts from the Quran, honors authentic scholarship, and serves believers everywhere.

**Website**: https://www.deenup.app
**App Store (iOS)**: https://apps.apple.com/tn/app/deen-back-daily-dua-dhikr/id6755876142

## Core Features

1. **24/7 Quranic-Cited Answers** — Ask any Islamic question and get answers rooted in Quran and authentic hadith, sourced from trusted scholars (not AI-generated opinions)
2. **Daily Quranic Verses** — A new verse each day with contextual insights to reflect on
3. **Daily Duas** — Curated supplications for every moment of your day
4. **Quran Reading with AI Insights** — Read the Quran with contextual, AI-powered insights that help deepen understanding (not interpretation — always defers to scholars)
5. **Daily Habit Tracking** — Track your Islamic practices aligned with your faith goals
6. **Reflection Quizzes** — Deepen your understanding of Quranic teachings through guided quizzes

## CTA Writing Rules

**Tone**: Natural, helpful, never salesy. The CTA should feel like a friend recommending something genuinely useful.

**DO**:

- Tie the CTA directly to the article topic
- Use the specific feature that's most relevant
- Frame as "you might find this helpful" not "download now"
- Mention a concrete benefit the reader gets

**DON'T**:

- Use generic "download our app" language
- Force a CTA where it doesn't fit
- Promise things the app doesn't do
- Use marketing buzzwords or hype

**CTA Examples by Topic**:

For a dua article:

```
title="Never miss your daily duas"
blurb="DeenUp sends you personalized dua reminders throughout the day — morning adhkar, evening prayers, and duas for every situation."
```

For a "how to pray" article:

```
title="Build a consistent salah habit"
blurb="Track your daily prayers with DeenUp and build streaks that keep you motivated. Start with one prayer and grow from there."
```

For an "is X haram" article:

```
title="Get Quran-based answers to your questions"
blurb="Wondering about Islamic rulings? DeenUp gives you 24/7 answers rooted in Quran and authentic hadith from trusted scholars."
```

For an explainer article:

```
title="Deepen your understanding of the Quran"
blurb="Explore Quranic verses with AI-powered contextual insights and daily reflections — all grounded in authentic scholarship."
```
